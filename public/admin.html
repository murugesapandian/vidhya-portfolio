How publishing works

  1. Save Changes stores your edits in this browser so you can preview them on the site instantly.
  2. To make changes permanent for everyone, click Download artwork.json.
  3. Put the downloaded artwork.json and any new images (in the artwork/ folder) into the project, then push to GitHub. The site updates automatically.
