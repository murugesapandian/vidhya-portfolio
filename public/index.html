About the Artist

Vidhyasree M.

Hello, I'm Vidhyasree.

I'm a young digital artist and illustrator. I create all of my work on an iPad, building each piece from color, light, and emotion.

My work often plays with contrast — bright color against grey, joy against stillness — and tries to pull the viewer into the worlds I imagine. Many of my pieces are about feeling: how it looks when it spills out, glows, or quietly fades.

This portfolio is a growing collection of the work I love most. Thank you for visiting.
